This is an iTec themed creature collector which will grow over time with contributions from students in the iTec Program. Each year, the secondary 1 students will design their own creatures, and the secondary 5 students will collaboratively design a region, so the game can continue to expand and be influenced by each cohort.

Please follow the main design outlined in the GDD. Everything is open for collaboration by iTec student & staff, so feel free to reach out through Teams or Discord if you'd like to add or change something in the project, or if you'd like to take on the role of completing a specific aspect of the game.

See contribution.md for a step-by-step on how to contribute to the project

See tecmon_design_guidelines.md for the rules related to designing your own tecmon.
