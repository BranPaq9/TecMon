This is an iTec themed creature collector which will grow over time with contributions from students in the iTec Program. Each year, the secondary 1 students will design their own creatures, and the secondary 5 students will collaboratively design a region, so the game can continue to expand and be influenced by each cohort.

Please follow the main design outlined in the GDD. Everything is open for collaboration by iTec student & staff, so feel free to reach out if you'd like to add or change something in the project, or if you'd like to take on the role of completing a specific aspect of the game.
